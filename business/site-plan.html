Home page

Harmony oaks
Welcome to Harmony Oaks , your destination for exquisite wood and epoxy creations! Step into a world where craftsmanship and innovation unite to deliver stunning functional art. Our artisans blend nature's finest wood with mesmerizing epoxy, crafting unique, durable pieces that captivate the eye and elevate any space. From striking river tables to intricate cutting boards, our collection showcases the perfect fusion of wood and epoxy. Whether you seek a centerpiece for your home or a thoughtful gift, we invite you to explore our range of handcrafted treasures. Embrace the allure of wood and epoxy, and make your space a testament to elegance and style.

Experience the multitude of benefits that Harmony Oaks Crafts brings to your life. Our handcrafted excellence ensures every piece is a work of art. You'll find unique, customizable designs that add timeless elegance to your space. Our commitment to using durable, quality materials ensures your investment will stand the test of time. We believe in artistry meeting functionality, creating pieces that not only look stunning but also serve a purpose. With a focus on sustainability and exceptional customer service, you can trust us to provide not just products, but an experience that enriches your life.

What is our Goal We are a group of friends that has enjoyed working with each other on different projects and we want to share that with others by selling some of our projects and using that money to fund some bigger projects we would like to try and make a river table in the future if we are able to reach enough people

At Harmony Oaks Crafts, we take pride in crafting wood and epoxy creations that resonate with the beauty of nature. Our commitment to quality, creativity, and customization ensures you'll find the perfect addition to your home or a unique gift that will be cherished for years to come. Explore our gallery and let us bring a touch of the outdoors indoors, transforming your living spaces into showcases of natural elegance.

The Team

Meet the team
Our group of friends has always enjoyed working on crafty projects. Danny and Kade have both done booths at the local renaissance fair and we all liked the idea of selling some of our projects. We are hoping to work together to make more unique merchandise.

Kade- I am the one that invited all my friends to join me in selling our projects and enjoy building things together. Some of my favorite projects are woodwork. John and I usually work together on things like tables, chairs, ect.. For my smaller projects I like to do cutting boards, clocks, and some origami.

Jonathan- I have recently discovered epoxy and have been doing a lot of projects with it. I started Mostly with some dice for my D&D games I was able to sell a few sets to other players I am starting to do some bigger projects like tables and I have a few other ideas about some new things to try.

Danny- I am the oldest of the group and plan to join the military in a few years. I enjoy doing leatherwork and have sold some at one of the renaissance fairs I have been to and I thought it was a great way of doing the things that I like to do
